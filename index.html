Pathway Sprints & V2MOM Drafting

11:15 – 11:45
Pathway deep dives
Each working group reads pre-drafted V2MOM stimulus for their 3 assigned boxes — 10 mins per box to absorb and take notes.
11:45 – 14:00
Working lunch + V2MOM refinement (AI-assisted)
3 × 35-min sprints per group: vision refinement → values & methods → obstacles & measures → prioritise 1–3 actions and set RAG readiness rating. Gemini captures and drafts a bespoke V2MOM live.
14:00 – 14:15
Afternoon tea
Part 3
Cross-Functional Design Critique
14:15 – 17:30
14:15 – 14:45
V2MOM playbacks
Each team presents their 3 boxes: RAG ratings, top dependency, and single highest-priority action.
14:45 – 15:45
The design review — Tribe & Guild model
Each team stress-tests another pathway as an outside "Guild" — naming hidden dependencies, challenging priority actions, and rating RAG honesty. Tech reviews Executive · People reviews Tech · Exec reviews People.
15:45 – 16:15
Critique playback to owners
3 × 10-min sessions — 5 mins Guild feedback, 5 mins owning team response. Facilitators capture all findings.
16:15 – 16:45
Critical path sprint
Dot vote on the 3 load-bearing boxes, then a 10-min stress test per box: "It's 18 months from now and we've failed at this one thing — how bad is it?"
16:45 – 17:15
Sequencing & dependency conversation
Align on owners, definitions of done, and timing across all priority actions to produce a 90-day sprint.
17:15 – 17:30
Reflections & close
Closing remarks and summary of actions.
